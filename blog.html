Bapuji Institute of Hi-Tech Education (BIHE) is a well-known educational institution in Davangere, Karnataka. It is part of the Bapuji Educational Association, which manages a variety of schools and colleges in the region, focusing on providing quality education across different disciplines. Here are some key aspects of BIHE:


BIHE is located in Davangere, a prominent city in Karnataka. The city is well-connected by road and rail, making it accessible for students from various regions.
